\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A 4D vector with x, y, z, and w components. 

This structure represents a 4D vector, commonly used for homogeneous coordinates in 3D transformations. It can be constructed from a \doxylink{structs21_1_1Vec3f}{Vec3f}, setting w to 1.\+0f by default.
